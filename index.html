About

I am an RTG Postdoc at UVA. Previously, I was a graduate student at the University of Chicago working with Shmuel Weinberger. Before that, I was an undergraduate at Cornell working with Jason Manning.

My CV

Research Interests

  • Smoothing theory
  • Group actions on high dimensional manifolds
  • Geometric group theory (specifically how it interacts with group cohomology)

  • Cohomology and the Bowditch boundary. (with Jason Manning) Michigan Mathematical Journal, 2020
